About Mutual Aid Schemes

Mutual Aid Schemes provide a way for communities to pool resources for emergency water deployment. Benefits include:

  • Cost sharing between participating entities
  • Priority access to emergency water bowsers
  • Reduced response times during emergencies
  • Financial assistance for underfunded areas

The contribution amount is the standard fee that participating entities will pay. When creating a new scheme, you can set an initial balance if funding has already been secured.
